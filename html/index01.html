About Us

As a specialist provider of high quality fruit to offices, you can be guaranteed of an efficient and reliable service that is convenient to you, and can be enjoyed by everyone.

Fruit sourcing

We source most of our fruit from the UK and Western Europe to help support UK producers and minimize fuel emissions from long-haul transportation.

Tree planting

For every fruit basket we sell – we plant 1 fruit tree in Malawi, Africa (together with UK charity Ripple Africa).
